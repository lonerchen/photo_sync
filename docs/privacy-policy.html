PhotoSync Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

PhotoSync is a local-network photo transfer app that helps users back up photos from mobile devices to their own desktop storage server. We respect your privacy and design the app to minimize data collection.

1. Data We Access

  • Photo Library: Used only to let you select photos/videos to upload, and restore media back to your library.
  • Local Network: Used to discover and connect to your PhotoSync storage server on the same LAN.
  • Device Basic Info: Device name/model/platform may be used for device identification in your own server UI.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not run a cloud service for your media uploads.

PhotoSync does not sell user data and does not upload your photos to third-party cloud servers by default.

3. How Data Is Processed

  • Media transfer happens over your local network between your phone and your own desktop server.
  • Transferred files are stored on your chosen local storage path.
  • App metadata (such as recent server connection records) is stored locally on your device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with advertising networks or data brokers. Data may only be transmitted to destinations you explicitly choose (for example, sharing a local installer file).

5. Security

We implement resumable transfer and local-only design principles to reduce accidental loss and unnecessary external exposure. You are responsible for securing devices within your own local network.

6. Children

PhotoSync is not specifically directed to children under 13.

7. Contact

If you have privacy questions, please contact support: djczzrqsglzs@gmail.com

For bug reports and suggestions, please use GitHub Issues: https://github.com/lonerchen/photo_sync/issues

PhotoSync 隐私政策（简体中文）

更新日期：2026 年 4 月 2 日

PhotoSync 是一款局域网照片传输应用，用于将手机照片备份到用户自己的桌面存储端。 我们重视隐私，并尽量减少数据采集。

1. 我们会访问的数据

  • 相册权限：仅用于选择照片/视频上传，以及回拉保存到系统相册。
  • 本地网络权限：仅用于发现并连接同一局域网内的存储端。
  • 设备基础信息：设备名称/型号/平台用于在你的存储端界面中区分设备。

2. 我们不会采集的内容

我们不提供默认云端照片托管服务。

PhotoSync 不会默认把照片上传到第三方云，不会出售个人数据。

3. 数据处理方式

  • 媒体传输发生在你的手机与自有桌面存储端之间（局域网）。
  • 传输文件存储在你配置的本地存储目录。
  • 应用元数据（如最近连接记录）仅保存在本机。

4. 数据共享

我们不会向广告网络或数据中介共享你的个人数据。 数据仅会在你主动触发的分享行为中发送到你指定目标（例如分享安装包）。

5. 联系方式

隐私相关问题请联系： djczzrqsglzs@gmail.com

问题反馈与功能建议，请使用 GitHub Issue： https://github.com/lonerchen/photo_sync/issues
